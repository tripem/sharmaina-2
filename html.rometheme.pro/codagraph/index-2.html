HI I'M SHARMAINA

Professional Event staffing coordinator

RoyalsRoyals is a modeling company that I opened at the age of 20 as an ushering concept,  we then advanced Into Royals this year, where now I aim to not only offer staffing services but teach everyone we work with about the beauty of being African.
Through Royals, we have embraced a diverse range of methods to express the rich heritage and timeless elegance of Africa. We combine modern style with traditional elements, celebrating the royalty that lies within our culture. Whether it’s through the vibrant patterns in our attire, the regal poise of our models, or the storytelling woven into our events, we proudly showcase Africa’s kingship and rich heritage.

Our work is more than just about staffing; it’s about empowering others to understand and take pride in their roots. We aim to redefine what it means to be African by blending contemporary sophistication with deep cultural significance. Royals stands as a beacon of African excellence, where every individual we work with is a testament to the grandeur and legacy of our continent.
